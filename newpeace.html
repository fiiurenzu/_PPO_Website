The studio collaborated with International contemporary artist Timur Si-Qin for the occasion of his Solo show “Take Me, I Love You” at the VonAmmon co gallery in (Washington DC, USA). Where design studios such as ours are usually commissioned to work on the visual Identity of Art Centers and Museums, framing & gravitating around the works of the featured artists, our input was meant to be fully integrated at the core of his sculptures and installations. We immediately loved the idea that Timur Si-Qin was completely reconsidering the role of graphic design within an exhibition space and its relation to Art and objects. It appeared to us as such an atypical and unprecedented assignment as well as an innovative work process.
Our mission was to work on an unseen series of pieces presented for the occasion by the artist. Under Timur’s creative direction, we have worked to define, develop & enrich the visual grammar and graphic line of “EDGELIT”, a collection of 4 sculptures/ installations mixing numerous printing and fab technics as 3D/UV print, Laser cut/engravings, LED lights systems. Displaying our Graphic & type design skills, we were asked to enrich the already existing design codes and systems set up by Timur Si-Qin during his past exhibitions. The first step of this process was to develop an exclusive new typeface that would fit Timur’s concept. His interest for natures signs and patterns was one of the key ideas that we followed to come up with the shapes that would compose the letters. In a second time, we assisted the artist designing and displaying the graphic elements on the 4 sculptures.
Timur’s statement comprises four modules of ideas, which became our working basis. These concepts are possible sources for basing a contemporary sprirituality in the face of climate change : Undivided Ground (Immanence), Faith In Pattern (Emergence), Pray to Scale (Higher power) & Purpose From Difference (Contingency).
