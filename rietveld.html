Gerrit Rietveld Academie

Graduation event

GRA Diploma 2020

Amsterdam, NL 2020
Services : Graphic design, Design & development

Design of the diploma for the 2020 graduates from the Dutch University of applied sciences for Art & Design, located in Amsterdam. The project consisted in delivering a range of standardized documents to the school administration so that the in-house team can edit the versions according to the different concerned departments, juries and students. Both the typographical part and the still-life illustrations were made by using a modular grid.
