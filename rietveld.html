Gerrit Rietveld Academie

Graduation event

Diploma 2020

Amsterdam, NL 2020
Services : Graphic design, Design & development,

The studio was commissioned to design the diploma of the 2020 graduates from the Dutch University of applied sciences for Art & Design, located in Amsterdam. This administrative design project consisted in delivering a range of standardized documents to the school staff so that they can edit them with the different concerned departments, juries and students. Both the typographical part and the still-life illustrations were made by using a modular grid.
