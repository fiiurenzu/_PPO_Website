PPOffice (Panama Papers Office) is a creative studio founded in 2019 by Pierre Dufresne & Florent Martini, joined by Étienne Clerc, art director, in 2021. Working both locally and internationally, our core team is dedicated to develop bold creative guidelines, visual narratives and graphic design systems with the precious expertise of various freelance designers familiar with the studio and respectively evolving in diverse creative practices. Our works are always based on the strength of a solid prior semiological approach and research stage in line with each project, its context and purposes.
