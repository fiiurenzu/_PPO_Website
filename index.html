About

PPOffice (panama papers.office) is a creative studio established in 2019 by Pierre Dufresne, Ilan Begarin & Florent Martini, joined by Etienne Clerc in 2020. The studio is developping creative systems, images, design and consulting.

Selected Collaborators

Sony Music (SW), Timur Si-Qin (USA), Vonammon Gallery (USA), University Le Havre Normandie (FR), Gerrit Rietveld Academy (NL), Ada Sokol (PL), Tunica Magazine (USA), Stedelijk Museum (NL), Entercourse of The New Age (FR), Edwin (EU), WWMM Magazine (CH)
